FAQ

 How to purcase from BredanMcCabe.com?

Just click on the artwork of interest and send us your offer via the "Make an offer" button.
Please do include the name of the artwork and offer, you can also use this form to ask for more information regarding the artwork(s) of interest.

Items on hold?

Items marked as "on hold" are temporary taken off the gallery untill all ongoing consultation and interests are concluded.

Shipping?

We will ship any purchse worldwide to the location as requested by the buyer, We only use shipping companies specialised in the Arts industry with suitable insurance policy in consultation with the buyer.

Returns?

Returns is not possible under any circumstances, At the discretion of the management some items may be re-exhibited on this website or any of BredanMcCabe,com exibitions for resale with terms in consulation.

Are the artworks on this site original?

Yes, all artworks on this website and in Bredan McCabe gallery are all original, one of a kind  pieces.
